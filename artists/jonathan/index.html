Jonathan




Jonathan
's work first appeared in the now seminal UK comics Deadline and Tank Girl back in the mid 90s. Since then he has produced illustrations for magazines such as Q, Mixmag, Mojo, MAD and been a weekly contributor to the UK newspaper, the Guardian, for the last 10 years.

He's also illustrated record covers for artists such as the Black Eyed Peas and the Jungle Brothers, had comics published in the UK, France and the USA and designed a range of t-shirts and hoodies for Extreme Raw State. He lives in a house full of plastic pop cultural ephemera with his other half, Louise "Felt Mistress" Evans.
