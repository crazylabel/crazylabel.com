Gavin Strange




Gavin Strange
is a jack of all trades; Senior Online Designer at Aardman Animations by Day and a designer going under the alias Jamfactory by night. Working in the field of Web & Graphics primarily but also character design, photography and illustration.

Gavin has created pretty things for the BBC, Channel 4 and Realmac Software as well as giving talks at Apple Stores around the UK. Droplet was his first venture into the world of vinyl and hopes there's many more toy adventures to come!
