Tweedlebop




Michael Fleming
graduated from CalArts with a BFA in experimental animation, and has been drawing on surfaces since the age of 3. He believes everyone should have a large round cartoon head.

Michael has worked with numerous clients over the years, including Scholastic, Arnold Worldwide, Eat Your Lunch LCC, Curious Pictures, Porchlight Animation, American Greetings and many more.
