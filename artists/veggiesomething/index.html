James Liu




Born in Pingtung, Taiwan and based out of Chicago, Illinois in the USA,
Veggiesomething
has loved art and design for as long as he can remember. His artworks have been seen throughout the world via numerous art shows and exhibits and have been featured in various publications and media outlets.

Thus far, Veggiesomething has created five character lines (FIZZIEFUZZIE, House of Liu, United by Destruction!, Sugar Bandits, and Debonair Rascal) and one lifestyle concept label (Killerslayer). These creative endeavors highlight his love for modular designs that are bold, clean, clear, and concise.
